How Much Fun Can It Really Be?

Web site contains examples of fun things to try while on an elevator:

  1. Blow your nose and offer to show the contents of your Kleenex to other passengers.
  2. Whistle the first seven notes of "It's a Small World" incessantly.
  3. Stand silent and motionless in the corner, facing the wall, without getting off.
  4. Frown and mutter "gotta go, gotta go" then sigh and say "oops!"
  5. Say "Ding!" at each floor.
